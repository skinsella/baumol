Labour cost breakdown by sector

Where Irish labour costs sit, by NACE Rev. 2 letter sector, and how they have moved. Source: CSO EHQ03 (Earnings, Hours and Employment Costs Survey), {{ data_period }}.

Hourly labour cost is the most directly Baumol-relevant series: it captures wages plus employer social-security contributions and other non-wage costs, normalised per hour worked. Quarterly data start in 2008Q1 — the pre-2008 NACE Rev. 1 series does not splice cleanly. NACE A (agriculture) and parts of D, E sit outside EHECS coverage and are omitted throughout.
